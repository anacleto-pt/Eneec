## ğŸ¯ About the Event

**ENEEC** is an event dedicated to promoting the Computational Engineering course at the University of Aveiro.

The mission is to:

  * Connect students with companies in the field.
  * Showcase the various applications of computational engineering.
  * Create networking and internship opportunities for students.

### Event Details

  * **Date:** February 11th & 12th, 2026
  * **Location:** Department of Physics, University of Aveiro

The website is a single-page application that includes:

  * **About:** Mission statement and event details.
  * **Program:** A full two-day schedule of talks, workshops, and networking events.
  * **Gallery:** Photos from previous editions (2022, 2023).
  * **Team:** The organizing team for the 2026 edition.
  * **Registration:** A form for attendees to sign up.
